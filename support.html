Common questions

How do I play?

Tap and hold the left or right half of the screen to steer Mahmut through the gap in each row of mountains. Let go to fly straight. Clip a peak and the run ends.

Can I reset my high score?

There's currently no in-app reset option. Your best score and recent runs are stored locally on your device — deleting and reinstalling the app clears them.

Does Mahmut show ads?

No. Mahmut has no ads, no accounts, and no in-app purchases.

What's the App Tracking Transparency prompt for?

It's used only to measure whether people who saw a Mahmut ad on Facebook or Instagram went on to play the game. Declining it doesn't change anything about how the game plays. See the Privacy Policy for the full details.

What devices does Mahmut support?

iPhone, running iOS 16 or later, in portrait orientation.

I found a bug — what should I include in my report?

Your iPhone model, iOS version, your score/what you were doing when it happened, and a screenshot if you have one. Email it to the address above.

Mahmut is an independent project. Response times may vary, but every email gets read.
